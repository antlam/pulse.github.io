Introducing Pulse

Insight without the spreadsheet.

Jane Pulse turns your data into plain-language insights on revenue, scheduling, and retention.

See your insights

Overview

Your data tells a story. Pulse reads it for you.

Upload a CSV from any practice management system and get AI-generated insights across revenue, scheduling, and patient retention — in plain language, in minutes.

Here's what Pulse surfaces:

Produced vs. collected revenue, outstanding balances, and collection rates
No-shows, late cancellations, and peak scheduling hours
Patients who haven't booked in 60+ days — before they find someone else
Plain-language summaries — no spreadsheets, no data expertise required

Know exactly where your money is going

See produced vs. collected revenue, outstanding balances, and collection rates — all in one view. No more guessing why the numbers don't add up.

Find the patterns eating your schedule

No-shows, late cancellations, peak hours — Pulse surfaces what's costing you time and money, so you can act on it instead of just absorbing it.

Bring back patients before they're gone

Pulse flags patients who haven't booked in 60+ days and gives you the insight to re-engage them — before they find someone else.
